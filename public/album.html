BIBI (비비), born on September 27, 1998, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and rapper who debuted in 2019 under Feel Ghood Music Known for her distinctive blend of R&B, hip-hop, and pop, BIBI has quickly gained recognition for her unique sound and artistic flair.
